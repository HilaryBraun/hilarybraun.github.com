Travel Poster and Schedule

While designing a travel poster for Greece, I was inspired by the images of all the unique local cuisine. Looking to attract travelers seeking an experience beyond tourist attractions, I created a poster that invites the viewer to make their experience a true adventure. The second part of this project was to take an existing train schedule and make a pocket-sized version which corresponds with our travel poster. I wrote the additional text, created all additional maps and imagery and based Attica Railways on one of the first railway systems in Greece. The poster is 16.5"x 25.5", the travel schedule is 2.75"x 4.25"; both are designed using Adobe Illustrator CS5 and Adobe InDesign CS5; both are printed on matte paper; the travel schedule is double-sided and folds 3 times into an accordion format.
